    Introduction

    Unity Mons is a free-to-play, casual monster collection game that aims to provide a high-quality, engaging gaming experience to players around the world. The game offers a refreshing alternative to popular games like Pokémon, by delivering a free and more casual experience that stays true to the heart of the monster collection genre, while incorporating modern gameplay elements.

    Problem Statement

    Many large game studios prioritize pleasing stakeholders, which often results in the release of incomplete and bug-ridden software. Furthermore, popular monster collection games like Pokémon can be costly, creating a barrier to entry for some players. Unity Mons aims to address these concerns by offering a free-to-play, casual monster collection game that is enjoyable for players of all ages and backgrounds, without sacrificing quality.

    Target Audience

    The target audience for Unity Mons encompasses individuals who enjoy Pokémon and other monster collection games but desire a more casual, free-to-play experience. The game is designed to be accessible to players of all ages and skill levels, featuring simple gameplay mechanics and easy-to-understand objectives, ensuring a fun experience for everyone.

    Platform and Format

    Unity Mons will be available as both a desktop and mobile application, ensuring that players can enjoy the game on their preferred devices, whether at home or on-the-go, providing convenience and flexibility.

    Main Components

    Unity Mons will rely on several key components, including advanced AI pathfinding, persistent collections/data for each player, an intuitive UI for managing creatures, and a casual yet engaging battle system. The game will also require skilled professionals in 3D modeling and animation, UI/Graphic design, programming/engineering, and data design. Moreover, the game will be developed using the C# programming language in the Unity engine, to ensure optimal performance, stability, and compatibility. I will personally handle the development of all programming/engineering and data design. I will outsource most of the 3D modeling and animation to third parties and will enlist co-developers for graphic design.

    Additional Features

    In addition to the main components mentioned above, Unity Mons could also offer multiplayer functionality, enabling players to battle against each other or collaborate to take on larger challenges. Another potential feature is a breeding system, allowing players to breed their creatures to create new and unique offspring, further enhancing gameplay variety. Unity Mons could also incorporate social media integration, empowering players to share their progress, achievements, and discoveries with friends and the wider gaming community.

    Timeline and Budget

    The development of Unity Mons will require substantial time and resources, with a projected timeline of 18-24 months from the start of development to the launch of the game. The budget will depend on the asset purchases required to build a diverse and captivating monster roster, ensuring the game remains fresh and exciting.

    Marketing Strategy

    To ensure Unity Mons reaches its target audience, a comprehensive marketing strategy will be essential. This strategy could include social media advertising, influencer partnerships, and targeted ads on gaming platforms. Establishing a strong online presence through a dedicated website and active social media accounts, along with engaging with the gaming community, will help build excitement and anticipation for the game's launch.

    Conclusion

    Unity Mons is a free-to-play, casual monster collection game that offers an enticing alternative to popular games like Pokémon. By providing a high-quality gaming experience that is accessible to players of all ages and backgrounds, Unity Mons has the potential to attract a large and diverse audience. With an array of potential features and a comprehensive marketing strategy, Unity Mons has the potential to become a cherished and successful title within the gaming industry.
